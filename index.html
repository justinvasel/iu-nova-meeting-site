Travel

  • Airport
    IND - Indianapolis International Airport

  • Getting to Bloomington

    There is a shuttle called GO Express Travel that goes from IND to Bloomington (and vice-versa). It takes 1 hour and costs is $20 per person. The shuttle departs for Bloomington hourly at 40 minutes past the hour. Find the schedule here.

    You should reserve a ticket on the shuttle before arriving HERE . If you don't, there is a "GO Express Travel" kiosk where you can buy them in-person (subject to availability, diagram here)

    When you retreive your luggage, follow the signs to "Ground Transportation" and meet the shuttle at ZONE 1 (here is a map).

    The shuttle driver will ask you for a confirmation number if you reserved online, or a ticket if at the kiosk. Be sure to tell them you're heading to the Biddle Hotel (also known as "IMU").

  • Leaving Bloomington

    Follow the same procedure as above. The shuttle will pick you up in front of the Biddle Hotel, and it's highly recommended you reserve your spot online ahead of time.

    NOTE The shuttle ride is usually 1 hour to the airpot, but there is a major construction project happening on that highway that can cause unpredictable delays. Give yourself plenty of time to get to the airport.
